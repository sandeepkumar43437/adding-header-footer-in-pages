Audeohost is a very powerful tool to include in your venue operator’s toolbox for venue and event promotion thereby maximising customer attendance and dwell time.

Audeohost’s award winning customer acquisition platform promotes all of your planned and, just in time (JIT) events in one place to attract both current patrons and new people to your venue.

App users will get to know about all of your events – from tv to trivia and more, and the time and place. Registered users who ♥ your venue see all of your events.

Every app user sees all planned events in the app and can complete a geolocation search and find out what is on in nearby venues or for a predefined distance from their current location.

With single or multiple onscreen events, your sound is ‘always on’ in the Audeohost app. When a show is transmitted in multiple languages, you can provide that too!

Hosting a private function or tournament known as an In-House Live event, the app has the details.

Customers can hear beyond the ambient music or the feature game when they want to hear commentary for something else currently onscreen. Audeohost creates multiple virtual zones.

For guests the connection is easy – connect to wi-fi – run the app – choose commentary from the audio menu and enjoy. A listening zone with a phone or bluetooth speaker is easy to setup – no wires, no fuss!
